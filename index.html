WINTER IS HERE!

Winter time is the best time to prune trees, and here's why...


Prevents the tree from being susceptible to insects and diseases which are also mostly dormant during the colder time of the year.

It is easier to see the overall form, structure, and any potential problems that need to be addressed.

Prevents storm damage to the tree causing further problems for the trees health.

ABOUT US

We care for your trees
as if they are our own

Our goal at Tree of Life Professional Tree Care is to properly prune your trees to ISA and ANZI 700 standards. We will make sure the work we do will promote the growth, health, and overall well being of your trees, and protect your property and loved ones.

We offer tree cabling, bracing, and removal services when necessary. Tree of Life Professional Tree care will plant 2 trees for every tree removed. Saplings will be donated to local schools to promote environmental awareness and a world our children will be proud of.
